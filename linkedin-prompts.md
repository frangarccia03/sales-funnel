## How to Use

1. Pick one prompt per day
2. Paste into Claude Pro
3. Edit the output in YOUR voice
4. Add YOUR real opinions/experience
5. Post on LinkedIn with the link to your funnel

The CTA at the end of every post should point to your sales funnel URL (where the chatbot lives), NOT directly to Gumroad. The chatbot qualifies them first, then recommends the ebook.
